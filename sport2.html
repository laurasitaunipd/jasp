Publication bias inflates effect sizes: a raincloud plot visualization

Raincloud plots illustrate how selective publication practices distort the distribution of reported effect sizes. The simulation parameters are inspired by Mesquida et al. (2022).

Raincloud Plots

Effect Size

The biased group shows larger and more dispersed effects, while the unbiased group is centered lower with less variability.This visualization combines raw data points, boxplots and smoothed density estimates, making the distortion introduced by bias both quantitatively and visually evident.
