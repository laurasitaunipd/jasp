Publication bias inflates effect sizes: a simulated funnel plot comparison

Funnel Plot

Publication bias condition = Present

Publication bias condition = Absent

Funnel plots illustrate how selective reporting of significant findings (biased sample) leads to asymmetric distributions and inflated effect size estimates compared to a scenario where all study outcomes are included (unbiased sample). Data are simulated based on parameters inspired by Mesquida et al. (2022).
